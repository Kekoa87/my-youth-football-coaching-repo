Offseason Gym Workout 02

This is a 5-day off-season gym workout plan tailored for a 13U linebacker, drawing inspiration from Coach Isaac Hadac's 5 Star Football Package and his emphasis on position-specific training. This program focuses on building strength, explosiveness, and agility—key attributes for a linebacker

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5
Day 1 – Lower Body Strength & Power

Back Squats, Romanian Deadlifts, Walking Lunges, Box Jumps, Calf Raises, Plank holds, & Russian Twists

Warm-Up

Dynamic stretches and mobility drills to prepare for explosive movements.

Back Squats 4x6

Romanian Deadlifts 3 x 8

Walking Lunges 3 x 10 (each leg)

Box Jumps 3 x 5

Calf Raises 3 x 15

Plank holds 3 x 1-minute

Russian Twists 3 x 20

Day 2 – Upper Body Strength & Mobility

Incline Bench Press, Pull-Ups, Dumbbell Shoulder Press, Bent-Over Rows, Face Pulls, Hanging Leg Raises, & Side Planks

Warm-Up

Arm circles, band pull-aparts, Push-ups: 2 sets x 15 reps

Incline Bench Press 4 x 6

Pull-Ups 3 x max

Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 x 8

Bent-Over Rows 3 x 10

Face Pulls 3 x 12

Hanging Leg Raises 3 x 10

Side Planks 3 x 30 seconds (each side)

Position Routine

Example: Linebacker

Play Football – LB Drills w/ Denzel Perryman to improve Reaction, Footwork & More!

Day 3 – Agility & Speed

Follow Coach Jamal Liggin workout.

Speed, Agility & Strength Drills

Day 4 – Functional Strength & Conditioning

Circuit: Kettlebell Swings, Push-Ups, Goblet Squats, Battle Ropes, & Farmer's Carry. Core: Plank with Shoulder Taps, & V-Ups

Warm-Up

Jump rope: 3 minutes

Dynamic Warm Up

Circuit (3 rounds):

Kettlebell Swings: 15 reps
Push-Ups: 20 reps
Goblet Squats: 15 reps
Battle Ropes: 30 seconds
Farmer's Carry: 40 yards

Core

Plank with Shoulder Taps: 3 sets x 20 taps
V-Ups: 3 sets x 15 reps
Day 5 – Recovery & Mobility

Light jog or cycling, Foam rolling, Static stretching, & Yoga poses focusing on hips and shoulders
