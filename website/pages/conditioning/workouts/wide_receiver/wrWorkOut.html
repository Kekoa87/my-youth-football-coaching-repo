WR Offseason Gym Workout

This is a 4-day weekly gym-based program tailored for youth WRs focused on improving explosiveness, upper/lower body strength, and football-specific movements. Each day below includes a sample workout video for guidance.

Day 1 – Lower Body Power & Acceleration

Hang Cleans, Trap Bar Deadlifts, Broad Jumps, Sled Pushes, Single-Leg Bulgarian Split Squats, Explosive Step-Ups, Stretch & Cool Down

Hang Clean 4x5

Trap Bar Deadlift 4x5

Broad Jumps 3x5

Sled Push 5x15-yards

Single-Leg Bulgarian Split Squats 3x8

Explosive Step-Ups 3x6 per leg

Day 2 – Upper Body Strength + Hands

Incline Bench Press, Pull-Ups, Barbell Rows or Dumbbell Rows, Standing Overhead Press, Dumbbell Chest Flys (control focus), WR Catch Routine, Core Circuit: Plank, Side Plank, Russian Twist.

Day 3 – Sprint Mechanics + Core

Focus on acceleration, sprint form drills, resisted sprints, and core development.

Day 4 – Explosive Movement + WR Footwork

Medicine ball throws, Olympic lifts, ladder and cone drills, and band-resisted routes.
