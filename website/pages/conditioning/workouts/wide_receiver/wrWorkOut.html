WR Offseason Gym Workout

This is a 5-day weekly gym-based program tailored for youth WRs focused on improving explosiveness, upper/lower body strength, and football-specific movements. Each day below includes a sample workout video for guidance.

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5
Day 1 – Lower Body Power & Acceleration

Hang Cleans, Trap Bar Deadlifts, Broad Jumps, Sled Pushes, Single-Leg Bulgarian Split Squats, Explosive Step-Ups, Stretch & Cool Down

Warm-Up

Dynamic stretches and mobility drills to prepare for explosive movements.

Hang Clean 4x5

Trap Bar Deadlift 4x5

Broad Jumps 3x5

Sled Push 5x15-yards

Single-Leg Bulgarian Split Squats 3x8

Explosive Step-Ups 3x6 per leg

Day 2 – Upper Body Strength + Hands

Incline Bench Press, Pull-Ups, Bent Over Rows, Standing Barbell Shoulder Press, Flat Dumbbell Flyes, Stretch & Cool Down

Warm-Up

Shoulder bands

Incline Bench Press 8x3

Pull-Ups 3x10

Bent Over Row 3x10

Standing Barbell Shoulder Press 3x8

Flat Dumbbell Flys 3x12

Core Circuit

Perform 2–3 rounds of this circuit: Plank, Side Plank (both sides), and Russian Twists.

WR Catch Routine

PlayFootball: WR Drills with Odell Beckham Jr.

Follow Coach Jamal Liggin workout.

Speed, Agility & Strength Drills

Day 4 – Lower Body Strength + Core Stability

Back Squat, Romanian Deadlifts, Partner Glute-Ham Raises or Nordic Curls, Dumbbell Step-Ups, Rotational Med Ball Slams, Cable Woodchoppers, and Ab Wheel Rollouts

Back Squat 4x8

Romanian Deadlift 4x8

Partner Glute-Ham Raise 4x8

Nordic Hamstring Curls 4x8

Dumbbell Step-Up 4x8 per leg

Rotational Med Ball Slam 3x10 per side

Cable Woodchopper 3x12 per side

Ab Wheel Rollouts or Hanging Leg Raises 3x8

Day 5 – Upper Body Explosiveness + Grip

Push Press or Landmine Press,

Push Press or Landmine Press, Medicine Ball Chest Pass (Explosive), Explosive Pushups, Rope Climbs or Rope Pulls (Grip Focus), Farmers Carries (Heavy Dumbbells/Kettlebells), Plate Pinches (Grip Strength), & WR Hand-Eye Catching Drill

Medicine Ball Chest Pass (Explosive) 3x10

Explosive Pushups 3x10

Rope Climbs or Rope Pulls (Grip Focus) 3x5

Farmers Carries (Heavy Dumbbells/Kettlebells) 3x40-yards

Plate Pinches (Grip Strength) 3x45 seconds

WR Hand-Eye Catching Drill
