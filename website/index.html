About Me

Hi, my name is Kyle. I’m excited to create this webpage as a valuable resource for coaching youth football. This platform will serve as a hub for drills, plays, and other coaching-related content, while also documenting my personal journey.

I’ve been coaching youth tackle football since 2021. Prior to that, I gained experience coaching various sports like flag football, basketball, T-ball, and coaches pitch baseball at our local rec center. When my eldest son expressed interest in playing tackle football, we enrolled him with the local club team known as the Hawks.

Through my involvement with the Hawks, I developed a deep appreciation for the art of coaching. Curious to learn more about coaching tackle football, I reached out to the team to inquire about my son’s head coach. To my surprise, they informed me that he didn’t have a coach yet and asked if I would be interested in taking on the role. After careful consideration, I reluctantly accepted the opportunity. Since then, coaching has been an absolute blast, and we’ve achieved success, which is always gratifying.

I’m thrilled to share my knowledge, experiences, and insights on this website to support fellow coaches and provide valuable resources for youth football coaching. Together, we can make a positive impact on young athletes and help them develop both on and off the field.
